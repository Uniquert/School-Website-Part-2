st xavier holy church

the school church was founded and established on may, 15th 1978.

St Xavier's Holy church is a very important peace in the communituy of the town of xavier. This town was so dedicated to their saint xavier that in 1977 they decided that every building in the future was going to have the word "xavier" in it. They started of with founding the memorable St Xavier Holy Church. Nowdays, St Xavier Holy church is known as the first building named after Xavier and will be remebered forever by the citzens of Xavier.



st xavier school library

the school library was built in 1980

Just like St Xavier Holy Church, the school library was an important piece of history, at first it started of as a school library for younger kids, but over time it became a place avaliable for all the citzens of the town of Xavier.



st xavier school aditorium

the auditorium was built in 1982

The auditorium was a memorable part of the history of the town of Xavier. It hosted many special events that were played by the school and even some from celebraties from across the globe. This audtorium started of as a school auditorium but after time it became a very popular place that held daily shows for tourists across the country to go to.
